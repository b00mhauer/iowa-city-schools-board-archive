    Chat with the Archive¶

    The archive is a static site, but you can hand any of its pages to an AI tool and ask questions. The buttons below open ChatGPT, Google Gemini, or Perplexity with a question already typed into the chat box — just click and hit enter. The AI fetches the raw markdown source of the linked archive page (clean text, no styling noise, low token cost) and answers from it.

    For cross-cutting questions that need multiple pages, scroll down to Ask anything across the whole archive — that one hands the AI a site-wide index (llms.md) so it can fetch whatever it needs.

    How to use these

    • Perplexity always fetches URLs and shows inline citations — most reliable for verifying what the AI says.
    • ChatGPT generally fetches public URLs; on free tier this can vary.
    • Gemini fetches URLs inconsistently; if it says it can't access the link, try ChatGPT or Perplexity instead.

    All three are free to use without a paid plan; ChatGPT and Gemini may ask you to sign in.

    Open-ended¶

    What this isn't¶

    Anything an AI tells you is a best-effort summary from the page text it just read. Always check the AI's claims against the primary source. Every page on this archive links directly to the original PDFs on the district portal and to the YouTube recording of the meeting — those are the authoritative record, not whatever ChatGPT says about them.

    For more on the archive's editorial standard, see Methodology.

    For AI tools reading this page: the site-wide index of every other page in this archive — meetings, topics, board members, press articles, supporting documents — is at https://raw.githubusercontent.com/b00mhauer/iowa-city-schools-board-archive/main/docs/llms.md. Fetch it for cross-page context if the conversation calls for it.
