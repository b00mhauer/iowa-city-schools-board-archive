Prepared by and return to: Kevin D. Olson, City Attorney, 1512 7th Street, Coralville, IA 52241, (319) 248-1700.
TEMPORARY CONSTRUCTION EASEMENT
THIS AGREEMENT, made and entered into by and between Iowa City Community
School District, an Iowa school corporation, 2255 N Dubuque Rd. , Iowa City, Iowa 52245,
hereinafter referred to as "GRANTOR," and the City of Coralville, a municipal corporation,
1512 7th Street, P.O. Box 5127, Coralville, Iowa 52241-5127, hereinafter referred to as "CITY."
FOR THE PARTIES' JOINT AND MUTUAL CONSIDERATION IT IS HEREBY
AGREED AS FOLLOWS:
1. THAT UNDERSIGNED CITY has requested an easement interest across certain real estate
described in Exhibit "A" attached to this Agreement and by this reference made a part hereof..
2. That the GRANTOR hereby grants and conveys to the CITY a temporary construction
easement in the area described and shown in Exhibit "A” attached here to in order to facilitate
the initial construction of the Forevergreen Road Extension – Phase 1(the “Project”).
3. . Said temporary construction easement shall terminate at the earlier of acceptance of the
Project by the City or September 1, 2027, whichever is earlier.
4. That CITY shall have the right to make excavations and to grade as it may find reasonably
necessary for the construction, operation, repair, maintenance and reconstruction of the Utility
Installation in the easement area.
5. That CITY shall have the right to trim and remove all trees and bushes which substantially
interfere with the exercise of the CITY'S rights pursuant to this easement; however, if valuable
timber is to be removed, it may be timely claimed as the property of the GRANTOR and
promptly removed.
6. That CITY shall have the right of ingress and egress to and from the easement area by such
route as shall occasion the least practical damage and inconvenience to the GRANTOR.
7. That GRANTOR reserves the right to use the above-described real estate for purposes which
shall not interfere with the CITY'S or public's full enjoyment of the rights granted in this
easement; provided, however, that the GRANTOR shall not erect or construct any building
or other structure, or drill or operate any well, or construct any reservoir or other obstructions
within the easement, nor shall GRANTOR allow or cause any substantial fill or cut over said
1

easement without the consent of CITY, which consent shall not be unreasonably withheld.
9. That CITY agrees to promptly repair any damages within the areas subject to the easement,
with the intent being to restore the surface of said areas to as close to original condition as is
reasonably practicable.
10. That CITY shall defend, indemnify, and hold harmless GRANTOR against any loss or
damage which may occur in the exercise of the easement rights by the CITY, its agents or
contractors, except for loss which may by occasioned by a diminution in business or
personal use during the temporary use of the area for construction, repairs, maintenance
and/or reconstruction in the easement area.
11. That the GRANTOR has the authority to grant the easement rights conveyed to the CITY herein,
subject to Iowa Code §§ 297.22 and 297.25.
12. That CITY will be responsible for replacing any property pins displaced as a result of
construction of the Project.
13. That this written Temporary Construction Easement Agreement constitutes the entire
agreement between GRANTOR and CITY and there is no agreement to do or not to do any
act or deed except as specifically provided herein.
