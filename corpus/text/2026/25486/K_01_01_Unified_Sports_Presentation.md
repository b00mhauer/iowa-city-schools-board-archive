What Is Unified Sports?
The Unified Sports Program brings students with and without disabilities together through
shared athletic and activity experiences. The program promotes inclusion, teamwork,
leadership, and meaningful relationships while ensuring equitable access to
school-sponsored activities for all students.
Unified Sports is more than competition; it’s about connection, respect, and belonging.
