RESOLUTION FIXING THE DATE FOR PUBLIC HEARINGS
ON THE PROPOSED DEPOSIT OF PROCEEDS FROM THE
SALE OF PROPERTY
WHEREAS, the District anticipates the sales of real property, consisting of the Hills
property, located at 301 W. Main Street Hills, Iowa; the Educational Services Center, located at
1725 N. Dodge Street; and approximately 42.4 acres of unimproved real estate previously
purchased from Scanlon Family, L.L.C (the “Properties”); and
WHEREAS, the District anticipates receiving proceeds from the sales of the Properties;
and
WHEREAS, Iowa Code §279.41(2) and §297.22(1)(b)(2) allows the board of directors of
a school district to deposit money received from the sale of schoolhouses, school sites, or both
schoolhouses and school sites into any account maintained by the school district after holding a
public hearing on the proposed action of the board; and
WHEREAS, Iowa Code §279.41(2) and §297.22(1)(b)(2) requires the board of
directors to publish notice of the time and place of the public hearing in the same manner as
required in Iowa Code §24.9.
NOW, THEREFORE, it is resolved:
1. Public hearings will be held in the Educational Services Center Board Room
located at 2255 N. Dubuque Rd., Iowa City, Iowa, on the proposed deposit of proceeds received
from the sales of the Properties.
2

2. The Secretary is authorized and directed to publish notices of the public hearings
in a newspaper having general circulation in the District. Such publication will be made not less
than ten nor more than twenty days ahead of the hearing date, and be in substantially the following
form:

OF PROCEEDS RECEIVED FROM THE SALE OF REAL
