RESOLUTION APPROVING THE DEPSOIT OF PROCEEDS
RECEIVED FROM THE SALE OF REAL PROPERTIES INTO
THE GENERAL FUND
WHEREAS, the District anticipates the sales of real property, consisting of the Hills
property, located at 301 W. Main Street, Hills, Iowa; the Educational Services Center, located at
1725 N. Dodge Street; and approximately 42.4 acres of unimproved real estate previously
purchased from Scanlon Family, L.L.C (the “Properties”); and
WHEREAS, the District anticipates receiving proceeds from the sales of the Properties;
and
WHEREAS, Iowa Code §279.41(2) and §297.22(1)(b)(2) allows the board of directors of
a school district to deposit money received from the sale of schoolhouses, school sites, or both
schoolhouses and school sites into any account maintained by the school district after holding a
public hearing on the proposed action of the board; and
WHEREAS, Iowa Code §279.41(2) and §297.22(1)(b)(2) requires the board of directors
to publish notice of the time and place of the public hearing in the same manner as required in
Iowa Code §24.9; and
WHEREAS, the Board of Directors proposes to deposit the proceeds received from the
sales of the above-mentioned Properties into the General Fund; and
WHEREAS, published as required by law, the Board of Directors held hearings on the
proposal to deposit proceeds from the sales of the Properties, and the extent of objections received
from residents or property owners as to said proposed transaction has been fully considered; and,
accordingly the following action is now considered to be in the best interests of the District and
residents thereof:
NOW, THEREFORE, be it resolved:
3

Section 1. That the entire amounts received from the sales of the Properties shall be
deposited into the General Fund.
Section 2. The Board President, Secretary, Superintendent, and administrative officers of
the District are authorized to take all actions necessary to complete the above-described action.
PASSED AND APPROVED this 26th day of May, 2026.
