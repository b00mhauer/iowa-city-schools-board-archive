Two (2) contract days each year shall be designated as "work from an alternative location."
These days shall be indicated on the staff calendar each year as "teacher work days" and
determined by the district's calendar committee. During these two teacher work days,
building- or district-directed professional development shall not be planned.
Employees shall be granted four (4) days leave per year for personal business at the
discretion of the employee. Employees may carry over a maximum of two (2) days of
personal leave to the following school year. Employees that end the year with a personal
leave balance of at least two days may cash in one unused day for $175 and still carry over
up to two personal days. Employees that have a personal leave balance under 2 days at the
end of the year are not eligible to cash in a day.
● An employee planning to use a personal day shall notify the employee’s supervisor
at least three (3) days in advance, except in cases of emergency.
Article XV – Employee Hours
Professional Development time on Thursdays, fourteen (14) times a year, including one
Thursday each month (September - May) will be designated as employee-directed
professional development. Any designated conference preparation days shall be included in
the 14 employee directed professional development days. During these designated
Thursdays, district-wide / school-wide PD will not be planned. Individualized PD must align
with District or building initiatives or the employee’s Career Development Plan (CDP).
Employees shall be allowed to work from an alternative location on these fourteen (14)
employee-directed professional development Thursdays. Should the district cancel school
or call an early release on a day designated for employee directed PD, the day will not be
shifted to a different Thursday. In addition to these 14 employee directed professional
development days, two Thursday professional development times shall be designated as
elementary conference dates.
