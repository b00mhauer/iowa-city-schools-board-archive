IOWA SCHOOL FOR THE DEAF
IOWA CITY SCHOOL DISTRICT
INTER-AGENCY CONTRACT FOR SPECIAL EDUCATION
2025-2026 SCHOOL YEAR
This agreement is entered into by the Iowa City School District, “resident agency,” and the State of Iowa,
Department of Education, for the benefit of the Iowa School for the Deaf, “provider agency”.
We, the undersigned agencies, for each special education student being provided services or programs by other
than the student's resident agency, hereby do consent and agree to the following conditions:
CONDITION I
The provider agency shall deliver Deaf Language Coach (DLC) services for the student referred in accordance
with the State of Iowa Department of Education Rules and Regulations and in accordance with state laws
governing such services and the delivery thereof (Chapters 256B, 28E, and 256).
CONDITION II
The cost of the DLC Services shall be paid by the resident agency to the provider agency in the total amount of
$8,640, for the students listed below. Payment of those costs will be determined and paid in the following
manner:
1. Costs will be billed as follows: $90 per hour, 480 minutes per month (September 2025 to January
2026) and 840 minutes per month (February 2026 to May 2026) pursuant to student IEPs. Costs will be billed
for $8,640 on June 1, 2026. Additionally, costs will be prorated if service minutes are amended. Service
minutes will be reviewed at least once annually. Costs will be prorated if DLC is absent and unable to
reschedule a session. Costs will be prorated if DLC sessions are canceled due to inclement weather or timely
notification of a student's absence, as set forth in Condition III.
2. Invoice shall be forwarded to the resident agency. The final payment shall be made no later than June
30, 2026, to the provider agency.
CONDITION III
1. Provider agency shall provide the resident agency with the following:
• A DLC for the number of minutes listed in the student’s IEP.
• Regularly updated logs detailing coaching activities.
• Detailed coaching lesson plans and materials required for the coach to provide lessons to the student.
2. Resident agency and/or parents shall provide:
• Space for the DLC to work with the student.
• Notification of student absence by 7:00 AM on the day of scheduled coach visits to avoid charges for
coaching services.
