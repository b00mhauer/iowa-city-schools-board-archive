Dear Curt:
On February 10, 2026, at 2 pm, we opened bids for the Track and Turf replacement work at West High School.
There were seven (7) bids received. Sprinturf of Daniel Island, South Carolina was the apparent low base bidder
at $1,768,070.00. There were two additive alternate bids, of which Sprinturf provided a price of $60,145 for
alternate 1 and $64,370.00 for alternate 2. Bray Architects recommends accepting the base bid and considering
the alternates.
While Bray has not worked with Sprinturf in the past we spoke with Iowa school districts who have recent
experience working with them and received good feedback. They were recommended by the individuals we
spoke with.
Therefore, we would recommend to the Iowa City Community School District that they approve the base bid in
the total amount of $1,768,070.00. With respect to the additive alternate, it would be a decision of the board as to
whether this should also be included in the contract for construction.
If you have any questions or need additional information, please feel free to call.
