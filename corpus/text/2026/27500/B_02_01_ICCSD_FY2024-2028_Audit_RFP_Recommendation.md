IOWA CITY COMMUNITY SCHOOL DISTRICT
AUDIT RFP RECOMMENDATION
RFP NUMBER: ICCSD-RFP-B-26001
The Iowa City Community School District published a request for proposals (RFP) for audit services on November
6, 2025, on the district website and sent notices to firms which had expressed interest. The district received three
qualified bids for audit services, with costs ranging from $70,000 to $86,000 for the FY2024 audit.
The district appreciates the interest of all three firms, and after extensive review, is recommending selection of
Bohnsack & Frommelt LLP as the district auditor for fiscal years 2024 through 2028. The proposal’s fee schedule is
as follows:
FY2024: $70,000
FY2025: $71,500
FY2026: $73,500
FY2027: $75,000
FY2028: $76,250
This proposal reflects a close alignment with the district’s goal of getting back on schedule with audits by March,
2027, at which point the FY2026 audit is scheduled to be completed. Further, Bohnsack & Frommelt LLP brings
substantial experience working with similar organizations, having served as auditor for districts including Waukee
CSD, Davenport CSD, Ankeny CSD, along with municipalities like the City of Iowa City.
We look forward to this engagement, and the aggressive approach that Bohnsack & Frommelt LLP has articulated in
terms of getting back on a regular audit schedule.
