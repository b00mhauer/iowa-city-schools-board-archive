## What happened

The May 12 meeting produced the **FMP (Facilities Master Plan) resolution**, drafted and motioned by Director Lingo — formalizing project-ranking and prioritization in capital work. Director Horn-Frasier was elected to the UEN (United Education Network) Steering Committee. Director Williams attended by phone from Denver (announced in advance at the April 28 meeting); Directors Eastham and Lingo experienced voting-system issues and verbally cast votes.

**Procedural setup for May 26.** This meeting **set the public-hearing date of May 26, 2026 for the resolution depositing ESC (1725 N. Dodge) sale proceeds into the General Fund.** That hearing and resolution were held and adopted at the May 26 meeting two weeks later. 59 supporting documents on the agenda.
