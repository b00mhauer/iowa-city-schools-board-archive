## What happened

A short special board meeting with one substantive action: **acceptance of the Special Education Director's resignation**. This was part of the broader cluster of senior-staffing changes that culminated in Superintendent Degner's May 23 resignation announcement and the May 26 board vote on his EDSS transition. Together with the new-CFO transition (Pat Moore started ~May 18) and the Interim Superintendent search underway, May 2026 was the month of compounding leadership change at ICCSD.
