To: Board of Directors
From: Matt Degner, Superintendent
Date: April 28, 2026
Re: Staff Anonymous Reporting Tool
As part of our continued efforts to enhance communication and transparency, the district is proposing a
staff-specific reporting option within the existing Say Something platform. This addition is being proposed
in response to requests for a clear, consistent, and confidential method for employees to submit concerns,
ask questions, or provide feedback.
This tool allows staff to submit reports anonymously. All submissions will be sent directly to the Ombuds,
who will review, assess, and determine any necessary follow-up actions. The process is designed to
encourage the submission of meaningful detail while preserving anonymity.
In addition, the tool provides information on how to contact the Ombuds directly, should that be the
preferred method.
A screenshot of the staff-facing interface is included below for your review.
This reporting option is intended to complement existing communication channels and provide staff with
an additional avenue for sharing feedback.
