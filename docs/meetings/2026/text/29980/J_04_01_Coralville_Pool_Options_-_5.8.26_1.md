which included over $100 million of projects. One of those projects was a collaboration with the City of
Coralville on a new Natatorium attached to the new Recreation Center. The school district’s contribution to
the project was $14,500,000 (includes contingency) which was developed utilizing an estimated cost model
that included 90% of the costs for the competition pool and 15% for the lap pool, as shown in the diagram
below (presented at the May 13th, 2025 Board Work Session).
