Biztown
Sixth-grade students have the exciting
opportunity to participate in Junior
Achievement’s BizTown, hosted at our
Center for Innovation. BizTown is an
immersive, hands-on learning experience
that allows students to step into a fully
interactive, simulated economy. Students
take on real-world roles as business
owners, employees, consumers, and
engaged citizens. They are responsible for
managing bank accounts, applying for
loans, running retail stores and restaurants,
and delivering services, experiencing
firsthand the challenges and rewards of
running a business and participating in a
functioning economy.
