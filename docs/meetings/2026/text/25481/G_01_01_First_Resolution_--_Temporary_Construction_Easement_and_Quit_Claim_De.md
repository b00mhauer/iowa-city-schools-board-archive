RESOLUTION
WHEREAS, the Board of Directors of the Iowa City Community School District has
received a proposal to convey interests in real property to the City of Coralville, Iowa, for good
and valuable consideration. The real property interests are a temporary construction easement
and quit claim deed located on property locally known as Van Allen Elementary School, 170
Abigail Avenue, North Liberty, Iowa, 52317 (the “Property”).
WHEREAS, it is appropriate pursuant to Iowa Code Section 297.22 to publish a Notice
of the proposed conveyance and of the hearing and to receive and consider objections and
petitions.
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Board of Directors of the Iowa City
Community School District:
Section 1. That this Board shall set a public hearing on the conveyance of the above-
described real property interests for January 13, 2026, at 6:00 p.m. in the Educational Services
Center located at 2255 N. Dubuque Road, Iowa City, Iowa 52245.
Section 2. That the Secretary is authorized and directed to prepare, publish, and distribute
the Notice of Hearing.
PASSED AND APPROVED this 13th day of January, 2026.
IOWA CITY COMMUNITY SCHOOL
DISTRICT
Ruthina Malone, Board President
