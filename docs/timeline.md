# Timeline

A chronological dashboard of the 2026 ICCSD board record. Each entry is one of three kinds:

- ***Board meeting:*** a meeting on the board calendar, linked to the meeting page on this archive.
- ***Reported:*** a news article published by a local outlet, linked to the original story.
- **Anchor events:** hand-curated pre-crisis facts and out-of-band events with source citations.

The Timeline is **auto-generated** from the press index, the meeting record, and the [anchor-events data file](https://github.com/b00mhauer/iowa-city-schools-board-archive/blob/main/data/anchor_events_2026.json). It refreshes daily as the press file is updated. **No interpretive characterization is added by the site** — entries carry the source's wording (article headline, meeting title) and the source's citation; readers draw their own conclusions.
