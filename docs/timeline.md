# Timeline

A chronological view of the major events the ICCSD Board acted on or
discussed during 2026. Each entry links to the meeting where the item was
on the agenda — open the meeting page for the underlying motion,
recommendation, and supporting documents.

For the full meeting-by-meeting roll-up, see the
[2026 meeting index](meetings/2026/index.md).

## 2026

### May

- **2026-05-26** — Board approved deposit of proceeds from the **sale of
  three real properties** (Hills property at 301 W. Main Street, the ESC at
  1725 N. Dodge Street, and ~42.4 acres of unimproved real estate
  previously purchased from Scanlon Family, L.L.C.) into the General Fund.
  Public hearings held same meeting.
  ([meeting](meetings/2026/2026-05-26-regular-meeting-of-the-board-of-directors-amended-agenda.md))
- **2026-05-26** — Five **negotiated agreements** taken up for FY26-27:
  Teachers (ICEA), Physical Plant (ICPPA), Secretaries (ICSUA), Nutrition
  Services (ICSNSA), and Paraprofessionals (ICEA). Three required action;
  two were year-2 informationals.
  ([meeting](meetings/2026/2026-05-26-regular-meeting-of-the-board-of-directors-amended-agenda.md))
- **2026-05-26** — Board discussed **Interim Superintendent Search**
  options (search firm proposal, comparison chart) — first publicly visible
  step in the leadership transition.
  ([meeting](meetings/2026/2026-05-26-regular-meeting-of-the-board-of-directors-amended-agenda.md))
- **2026-05-15** — Special board meeting accepted **Special Education
  Director's resignation**.
  ([meeting](meetings/2026/2026-05-15-special-meeting-of-the-board-of-directors.md))
- **2026-05-12** — Regular board meeting set the May 26 public hearing on
  the ESC (Dodge Street) property deposit; CFO Update presented.
  ([meeting](meetings/2026/2026-05-12-regular-meeting-of-the-board-of-directors.md))
- **2026-05-01, 05-05** — Opening proposals exchanged with three bargaining
  units (Physical Plant Custodians, Teachers, Secretaries) leading into
  the May 26 agreements above.

### April

- **2026-04-28** — Public hearing held on **conveyance of the ESC
  property** (1725 N. Dodge Street). Resolution authorizing conveyance
  passed same meeting.
  ([meeting](meetings/2026/2026-04-28-regular-meeting-of-the-board-of-directors-amended-agenda.md))
- **2026-04-14** — Board set the April 28 public hearing on the ESC
  property; 74 supporting documents on this single meeting — the
  highest-volume agenda of 2026 to date.
  ([meeting](meetings/2026/2026-04-14-regular-meeting-of-the-board-of-directors.md))
- **2026-04-01** — District announced **new Chief Financial Officer**
  (per district communications; not a board action).
