# Board Members

The Iowa City Community School District Board of Directors has **seven elected
seats**, each serving a four-year term. Election cycles are staggered so
roughly half the board is up for election every two years.

Roster as published on the district's official
[Board Members page](https://www.iowacityschools.org/our-district/school-board/school-board-members).

## What's on this page later

Individual board-member pages will eventually capture:

- Date first elected and election history
- Committee assignments (Education Committee, Policy & Governance Committee,
  Financial Oversight Committee, Legislative Forum representation)
- Motions made and notable votes, linked to the meeting page where each
  was recorded
- Public statements documented in board meetings

Until those are added, each member's name appears throughout the
[2026 meeting pages](../meetings/2026/index.md) — use site search (top bar)
to find every meeting that referenced a given member by name.

## Reporting a correction

If a name, email, or term-end year here is wrong, the district's official
page is the source of truth. Open an
[issue](https://github.com/b00mhauer/iowa-city-schools-board-archive/issues)
or a pull request and we'll re-sync.
