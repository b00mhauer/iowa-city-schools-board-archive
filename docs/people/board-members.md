# Board Members

!!! warning "Draft — pending editorial review"

    This page will list current ICCSD Board of Directors members with role,
    term, election year, and any notable committee assignments. It is
    intentionally not yet populated — board composition is a fact-sensitive
    page that the archive maintainer prefers to write by hand against the
    district's roster rather than scrape.

## Source of truth

For the current, authoritative board roster, see the district's
[Board of Directors page](https://www.iowacityschools.org/Page/2).

## How this page will work

For each current board member, this archive will provide:

- Role and current term
- Date first elected and election history
- Committee assignments
- Notable motions made and votes cast (linked to the meeting pages where
  they happened)

Past members will be listed below current members, with the period of
their service.

## Reporting a correction

If you spot something wrong on a member page, open an
[issue](https://github.com/b00mhauer/iowa-city-schools-board-archive/issues). Voting
records in particular should be 100% verifiable against the primary
sources.
