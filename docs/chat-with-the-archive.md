# Chat with the Archive

!!! warning "Coming in Phase 3"

    A NotebookLM-backed conversational interface is planned for a future
    phase of this project. It is **not live yet**. The links below are
    placeholders.

## What this will be

A separate, clearly-marked interface where you can ask natural-language
questions across the ICCSD board archive — every meeting, every supporting
document, going back several years — and get answers grounded in the
primary sources, with citations back to the exact PDF and page.

Example questions it should be able to answer:

- "Summarize how the board has discussed the superintendent search since
  the announcement of the previous superintendent's departure."
- "What policy changes touched discipline practices in the last two years?"
- "Find every meeting where the FY27 budget was discussed, and list the
  motions and votes."
- "Has the board ever publicly discussed school-resource-officer staffing?"

## Why a separate interface

A static archive (this site) and a generative-AI interface answer different
questions. The static archive is for verifying a specific known thing —
"what was the motion on the May 26 budget item?" — and is authoritative
relative to its sources. A generative interface is for exploring an unknown —
"what should I read to understand the board's stance on X?" — and is always
a best-effort summary, with citations you should follow.

Mixing the two would invite users to treat the AI's confident-sounding
summary as ground truth. They are not the same thing.

## Source of truth, always

Even when the chat interface is live, the rule from [How to Use](how-to-use.md)
stands: **the PDF on the district site wins**. The AI is a starting point.
Follow its citations. Read the originals.
