# Chat with the Archive

The archive is a static site, but you can hand any of its pages to an AI
tool and ask questions. The buttons below open **ChatGPT**, **Google
Gemini**, or **Perplexity** with a question already typed into the chat
box — just click and hit enter. The AI fetches the linked archive page
and answers from it.

!!! info "How to use these"

    - **Perplexity** always fetches URLs and shows inline citations — most
      reliable for verifying what the AI says.
    - **ChatGPT** generally fetches public URLs; on free tier this can
      vary.
    - **Gemini** fetches URLs inconsistently; if it says it can't access
      the link, try ChatGPT or Perplexity instead.

    All three are free to use without a paid plan; ChatGPT and Gemini may
    ask you to sign in.

## What this isn't

Anything an AI tells you is a best-effort summary from the page text it
just read. **Always check the AI's claims against the primary source.**
Every page on this archive links directly to the original PDFs on the
district portal and to the YouTube recording of the meeting — those are
the authoritative record, not whatever ChatGPT says about them.

For more on the archive's editorial standard, see [Methodology](methodology.md).
