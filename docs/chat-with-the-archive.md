# Chat with the Archive

!!! info "Coming soon"

    A NotebookLM-backed conversational interface for this archive is on
    the roadmap. The static site you're on now is the source of truth; the
    chat interface, when it ships, will be a separate tool that lets you
    ask natural-language questions across the corpus with citations back
    to specific PDFs and pages.

## What this will be

A separate, clearly-marked interface where you can ask natural-language
questions across the ICCSD board archive — every meeting, every supporting
document, going back several years — and get answers grounded in the
primary sources, with citations back to the exact PDF and page.

Example questions it should be able to answer:

- *"Summarize how the board has discussed the superintendent search since
  it was announced."*
- *"What policy changes touched discipline practices in the last two
  years?"*
- *"Find every meeting where the FY27 budget was discussed, and list the
  motions and votes."*
- *"Has the board ever publicly discussed school-resource-officer
  staffing?"*

## Why a separate interface

A static archive (this site) and a generative-AI interface answer
different questions. The static archive is for verifying a specific known
thing — "what was the motion on the May 26 budget item?" — and is
authoritative relative to its sources. A generative interface is for
exploring an unknown — "what should I read to understand the board's
stance on X?" — and is always a best-effort summary, with citations you
should follow.

Mixing the two would invite users to treat the AI's confident-sounding
summary as ground truth. They are not the same thing.

## Source of truth, always

Even once the chat interface is live, the rule from
[How to Use](how-to-use.md) stands: **the PDF on the district site wins**.
The AI is a starting point. Follow its citations. Read the originals.
