# Key Documents

A short, curated list of documents that come up repeatedly across topics —
the strategic plan, the current budget, the master facilities plan, the
board's bylaws and standing policies, the labor agreements in force.

!!! warning "Draft — pending editorial review"

    The "Key Documents" page is an editorial curation, not an exhaustive
    index. Curation is in progress; for the time being, browse the
    [2026 meeting index](../meetings/2026/index.md) and follow document
    links from there.

## How this page will work

Each entry will give:

- A short, plain-English description of what the document is and why it
  matters
- The most recent version, linked to the district portal where the
  authoritative copy lives
- Cross-references to the meetings where the document was discussed,
  amended, or adopted

## Browsing all documents in context

If you're looking for *every* attachment from every 2026 meeting, the
[2026 meeting index](../meetings/2026/index.md) is the right starting
point. Each meeting page links to every supporting document directly.
